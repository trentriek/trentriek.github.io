Forensic Nursing

This is a VR project in progress for the A&M School of Nursing. This is a trainsing simulator to teach Nurses the steps for conducting a forencis examination to collect evidence. There is a lack of material in the field and real practice examinations are not a reliable source of education due to the needss of volunteers or acutal patients.

My work on this project involed all elements of development. The traning simulater is seperated into modules which the user can select at will to choose training for. After inheriting this project from a previous developer, I worked with a collegue to build much needed backend structures to efficiently add new module courses. 

I then built 2 more modules, as well as worked closely with artists to implement assets and animations into the project. I packged the project, along the way finding solutions for issues related particularly to VR development. I am proud of this project and it's my most complete product to date.
