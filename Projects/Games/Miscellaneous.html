LightPhysics Prototype

This Project was abut utalizing light wavelength as a sort of tool for doing tasks - such as moving objects and so on. The next step in this process would have been to make the light more complex - currently it more or less works as one side of the spectum does one thing vs another. But in the real world, different lights are useful for different things. Purple light is the strongest wavelenth and red is the weakest. blue, red, and white light promote different growth patterns in plants, and green promotes no growth as its aborbe.d Red light is calm on our eyes, and so on. Many game mechanics can be built on this idea.
